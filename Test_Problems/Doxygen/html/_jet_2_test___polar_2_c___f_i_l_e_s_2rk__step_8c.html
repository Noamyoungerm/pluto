Limiter macros.

The following macros define a few limiter functions that can be used for piecewise linear reconstruction. Each limiter has the form SET_XX_LIMITER(dv, dvp, dvm, cp, cm) where dvp and dvm are the geometrically weighted forward and backward slopes ( and in Eq. [29]), cp and cm are weight coefficients depending on the distance between the cell interfaces and the centroid of volume (Eq. [33] in the reference paper). The limited slope (used in Eq. [30]) is returned in dv.
